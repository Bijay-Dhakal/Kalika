About Our School

Kalika Manavgyan Secondary School is one of the well-known educational institutions located in Butwal, Rupandehi district of Lumbini Province, Nepal. The school provides quality education from Class 1 to Class 12 (SLC level).

The school is affiliated with the District Education Office (DEO), Rupandehi and follows the national curriculum set by the Government of Nepal. With more than 8000 students and 300 dedicated teachers, the school has been serving the community for many years.

Our school has a library, science laboratory, computer lab, and a sports ground. We provide a safe, friendly and supportive environment where every student can grow academically and personally.

The school is located at Butwal-10, Ramnagar, which is easily accessible from different parts of Butwal city. We welcome students from all backgrounds and work hard to make sure every child gets the best education possible.
