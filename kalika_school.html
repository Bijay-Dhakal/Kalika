About Our School

Building knowledge, character and community since its founding in Butwal.

Kalika Manavgyan Secondary School is one of the well-known educational institutions located in Butwal, Rupandehi district of Lumbini Province. The school is committed to providing quality education from Class 1 through Class 10 (SEE level), shaping students with strong academic foundations and good character.

Our school follows the national curriculum set by the Government of Nepal and emphasizes both theoretical learning and practical skills. With experienced teachers, a supportive environment, and a strong community bond, students at Kalika Manavgyan grow to become responsible and capable citizens.

We believe every child deserves quality education regardless of background. Our dedicated staff works every day to make learning engaging, safe, and meaningful for all students.

Extra-Curricular Activities

We believe learning goes beyond textbooks. Our activities help students discover their passions and talents.

⚽

Sports & Athletics

Football, volleyball, cricket, athletics and annual sports meet. Students represent the school in district competitions.

🎭

Cultural Programs

Annual day celebrations, Dashain-Tihar programs, drama performances and traditional cultural events.

🎤

Debate & Speech

Inter-school debate competitions, public speaking workshops and elocution contests in Nepali and English.

🎨

Art & Drawing

Drawing competitions, poster making, rangoli and creative arts to develop imagination and artistic skills.

📝

Essay & Quiz

Essay writing competitions, general knowledge quizzes and inter-school academic olympiads.

🌱

Eco Club

Environmental awareness, tree plantation drives, cleanliness campaigns and nature education activities.

📖

Library Club

Reading programs, storytelling sessions, book reviews and developing a love of reading among students.

🤝

Student Council

Student leadership body that organizes events, voices student concerns and develops leadership skills.

🏕️

Educational Tours

Annual educational trips to historical and natural sites across Nepal for experiential learning.
