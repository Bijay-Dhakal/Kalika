Extra-Curricular Activities

At Kalika Manavgyan Secondary School, we believe that education is not limited to classroom learning. Extra-curricular activities help students discover their hidden talents, build confidence, develop teamwork skills, and become well-rounded individuals. We encourage every student to participate in at least one activity outside of regular studies.

Sports Activities

Activity Description When
Football Inter-class and inter-school football matches. Students can join the school football team. Year-round
Volleyball Volleyball practice sessions and competitions for both boys and girls. Year-round
Cricket Cricket matches organized within school and participation in district-level tournaments. Seasonal
Athletics & Running 100m, 200m, long jump and relay races for students of all classes. Annual Sports Day
Badminton Badminton for upper class students during lunch break and free periods. Year-round
Annual Sports Day A full-day sports event where all students compete in various sports. Parents are invited. Chaitra (every year)

Academic & Cultural Activities

Activity Description Frequency
Debate Competition Students practice public speaking and logical thinking through debates in Nepali and English. Twice a year
Essay Writing Essay competitions on important topics like environment, Nepal, and national days. Monthly
Quiz Competition General knowledge and subject-based quiz contests for all classes. Quarterly
Drawing & Art Drawing competitions, poster making and art exhibitions for creative students. Monthly
Cultural Program Song, dance, drama and cultural performances on annual day and national festivals. Yearly (Annual Day)
Science Exhibition Students make and present science models and projects. Best projects go to district level. Yearly

Clubs & Groups

Club Name What They Do
Student Council Student leaders elected from each class who help organize school events and represent student opinions to teachers.
Eco Club Tree plantation, school garden, cleanliness drives and environmental awareness programs.
Library Club Helps manage the school library, organizes reading programs and encourages students to read books.
Health Club Promotes hygiene, organizes health awareness campaigns and basic first-aid training.
Computer Club Students learn extra computer skills, typing practice and basic programming after school hours.
