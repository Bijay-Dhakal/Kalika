Welcome to Our School

Kalika Manavgyan Secondary School is located in Butwal, Rupandehi district of Lumbini Province, Nepal. The school provides quality education from Class 1 to Class 10 (SEE level). We are committed to building a bright future for every student through strong academics, good values, and extra-curricular activities.

Our school follows the national curriculum of the Government of Nepal and is affiliated with the District Education Office (DEO), Rupandehi. We have experienced teachers, a library, science lab, and a friendly learning environment.
